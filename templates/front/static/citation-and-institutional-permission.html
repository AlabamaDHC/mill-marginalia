Citation & Institutional Permission

All written material on Mill Marginalia Online—including editorial commentary, transcriptions, encoding strategies, and metadata schema—is available for free and unlimited use and reproduction to all visitors (Creative Commons license Attribution 4.0 International). A more limited permission is attached to the page image photos, which may be freely used for pedagogical purposes, but which may not be reproduced for publication in any form without written permission (Creative commons license Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivatives 4.0 International). Should you quote from or otherwise employ material from this site, please document your borrowings by original author (if provided) and title, site name, the project’s editors, your date of access, the URL, and the volume and page number (in the case of an unnumbered page, please note the location, i.e. “back flyleaf”).
